Account recovey

How to recover your account:

  1. Go to forgotten password page
  2. There fill the gap by putting your account email.
  3. If there is no error you will get an email of noreply@hexamarket.store, if the email isn't in the inbox look for it in the spam page.
  4. In the email you will see a dark blue button, click there.
  5. Then you will get to a page enter twice your new password

If you have questions contact us in our Discord.

Warning

The password must be the same in the two gaps. The password can not be the same as the old password.
