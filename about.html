About us

Hexamarket offers plugins ready to put on your server, to help configurators and owners of them, so that they can get ideas or basicaly use the plugin as it has been downloaded from our website. Hexamarket is a startup that dreams of becoming a big company and dedicating itself to something more than a forum where people can download their plugins and scripts configurated for their servers. Hexamarket since its inception has had a single programmer that is the manager. In addition to an administrator who has dedicated himself to making some "Scripts" for FiveM. Hexamarket has been in development since May, and was released on 12/10/2023. Hexamarket had a €0 budget for its creation, it's not much, but nobody wanted to risk losing €13 for a domain that lasts a whole year, in short, a shame for Hexamarket. Hexamarket has faced innumerable problems, for example: failure with the host, lack of knowledge to create something, problems with some pages, loss of all information and many more that I can't remember. We hope you can get the plugin already configured for your server, enjoy your stay around the web!
